Hollow Knight Save Editor

Open, edit, and export Hollow Knight and Silksong saves. Works with encrypted PC files and plain text Switch saves.

Expected save locations are listed below, but your files may still live somewhere else depending on launcher, cloud sync, or custom setup.

Silksong (expected)

  • Windows: %USERPROFILE%\AppData\LocalLow\Team Cherry\Hollow Knight Silksong\
  • Mac: ~/Library/Application Support/unity.Team-Cherry.Silksong/
  • Linux: ~/.config/unity3d/Team Cherry/Hollow Knight Silksong/

Hollow Knight (expected)

  • Windows: %USERPROFILE%\AppData\LocalLow\Team Cherry\Hollow Knight\
  • macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/unity.Team Cherry.Hollow Knight/
  • Linux: ~/.config/unity3d/Team Cherry/Hollow Knight/
  • Always keep a backup of your original save.
  • You can drag and drop a file anywhere on this page.
  • Right-click a history item to remove it.
